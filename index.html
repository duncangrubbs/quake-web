What is Quake?

Quake is an Android application that is made for both preparing yourself and knowing what's next for an earthquake.

Who made it?

It was developed by Duncan Grubbs (Programmer), Jessica Gerwin (Designer), Leo Krashanoff (Programmer), and Sebastian Boyd (Programmer) for the annual congressional app challenge.

About the App:

The current version of the app is supported on Android only but will be hopefully available for IOS soon. The app was developed over 2 months using Android Studio and Inkscape.

Quake is maintained by duncangrubbs.
